Design Background

Tea, since ancient times, has originated in China.

Tea culture has been prevalent in China for thousands of years and has never failed.

Nowadays, the fast-paced life makes people do not have enough time to enjoy the pleasure brought by brewing tea. The simplicity of the tea bag has replaced the complex process of traditional tea drinking. At the same time, the invention of the tea bag allows people to enjoy the fragrance of tea drinking and experience the charm of tea culture more quickly. Although the tea bag is easy to use, the correct brewing method can make people enjoy the delicious taste of tea more pleasantly and is beneficial to their health. Based on the above background, we have designed an intelligent tea brewer.

Function

Automatic lifting and pulling

The smart tea brewer can cultivate the habit of using tea bags correctly and enhance the taste of tea bags through the automatic lifting function.

When steeping the tea bag, you should gently lift and pull the tea bag to make it float up and down in the hot water, which can make the tea soup even. Tea bags should not be steeped all the time. Many people will steep the tea bags in hot water and drink them while steeping until they are finished, which is wrong. For example, if green tea is unfermented tea, take out the tea bag after putting it in for 30-60 seconds; while fermented tea, such as black tea, should be taken out after steeping for about 2 to 3 minutes. If you leave it all the time, the taste will deteriorate due to oxidation.

Speed Adjustment

The smart tea brewer can use the speed adjustment function to enhance the precipitation of nutrients in tea.

Tea bags can be brewed 2-3 times. Generally, when tea bags are brewed with boiling water, about 55% of the total soluble matter can be dissolved on the first brew; about 30% on the second brew; about 10% on the third brew; and only 1%-3% on the fourth brew. From the situation that the vitamins and amino acids contained in tea are leached out, 80% are leached out in the first brewing; the leaching rate reaches more than 95% in the 2nd brewing; the other active ingredients contained in tea such as tea polyphenols, caffeine, etc. are mostly the same.

The Procedure of Fabrication

2.Cut the PMMA with laser.

3.Assemble the PMMA to several parts and fix with hot melt glue.

4.Fix the arduino with the PMMA with the glue.

5.Assemble every parts to a product.


Significance

The smart tea brewer can make people use the tea bags more correctly through its automatic lifting function and speed adjustment function, and cultivate healthy habits of using tea bags. At the same time, the smart tea brewer can also enhance the taste and nutrient precipitation of the tea bags, making it easy and time-saving for people to enjoy the delicious taste of tea more pleasantly.
